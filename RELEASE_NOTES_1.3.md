# Manga Downloader 1.3

## English

### New and improved in this version

- Refreshed service selection screen:
  - service cards are fully interactive, click a card to open the selected source
  - cleaner animated glow style for a more polished look
- Cleaner navigation:
  - each downloader screen clearly shows the active service name in the top bar (`Manga.in.ua` / `Com-X.Life`)
- Faster access to key actions:
  - `History` and `Clear rows` are now in the top toolbar
- In-app guidance across the UI:
  - info popups added for save location, cover image, chapter and volume fields, turbo mode, Kindle mode, reset login (Com-X), and footer actions
- Better completion flow:
  - after all downloads finish, the app shows a clear success dialog with direct action: `Open manga folder`
- Visual stability fixes:
  - fixed unwanted focus highlight behavior on launch and footer controls
  - improved glow behavior for a more stable and less distracting UI
- Progress display consistency:
  - queue progress data is passed and displayed more consistently during active jobs
- Localization polish:
  - updated user-facing texts in English, Ukrainian, and Russian for better clarity and consistency
